Flat, cold and windy

It was cold last night!! To our surprise the temp this morning was 33°F. What happened to spring?

We spent the day covering miles towards Colorado where we have an appointment on Friday, and along the way we got a good dose of Kansas flat land. We found it interesting to compare the relative fertility of the country side with aridness of similar flat areas of inland Australia.
