Saturday 30th August
Day 2
Granville BC, 2nd and Pine
Miles: 123456
Lat: 45N
Long: 23W

We were up early and on our way by 7:30. The border crossing was very fast as there was no traffic going north. The same could not be said for the south bound though as there was a tremendous lineup -- it looked like about 3 hours.

We had arranged to have breakfast with John and Brenda Rogers (a couple we met on the central Asia trip in May/June) but before going to their place we decided to check our Granville Island; where the wedding was being help. A good thing too as parking was very tight. It was obvious that our original plan of parking on the island would not work and that some other option was needed.

We had a very enjoyable visit and breakfast with John and Brenda and caught up on our reflections on central Asia and some discussion about future travel plan. We left their place at 11:00

We found a street level parking lot about 1/2 a mile from Granville Island and as it turns out parked there all day.

Just as we were buying the ticket for the parking lot; a lady came by to look at the vehicle; it seems her husband is very interested in a Tiger but had never seen one in the flesh. So needless to say he came over (they lived close by) and had a look

The wedding went off well (from out perspective - will be interesting to hear latter what Gary and Yvonne thought on reflection) and we were back at the Tiger about 10:30pm

We parked in a near by street for the night (guerrilla camping - more on that latter) ... that worked out well
